🎮 Hotkeys (desktop)

  • Enter — roll / confirm placement
  • P — skip turn
  • R — rotate rectangle
  • W/A/S/D or arrows — move
  • Esc — settings
  • Q — manual
  • N — new game
  • L — switch language

📱 Touch controls

  • 👆 Tap on board — roll dice
  • 🖐️ Drag rectangle — move shape
  • 👆 Tap (no drag) — place shape
  • 📋 Menu ☰ — scores, turn, timer, actions

⚔️ Capture rules

Rectangle size exactly as dice. You can:

  • Occupy empty area (adjacent to your territory).
  • Capture enemy block entirely (if adjacent and correct size).
  • Capture neutral block (gray existing).

Covering an opponent's starting cell → opponent eliminated, their land goes to neighbors.

🏆 Victory conditions

  • Control ≥ 2/3 of the board
  • Eliminate all opponents
  • Board full → player with most territory wins
